About Us

Stone Cast Formations is a team of dedicated craftsmen and designers with a passion for creating timeless architectural elements in cast stone. With years of experience and a deep respect for both tradition and innovation, we specialize in producing custom mantels, moldings, and architectural features that enhance the beauty and value of any space. Our work is rooted in quality, precision, and a commitment to helping homeowners and builders bring their vision to life.

What We Do

Designing and Building
With Cast Stone

Fire Place Mantels

At Stone Cast Formations, we specialize in custom cast stone and wood-style mantels. Every mantel is made to order, tailored to your style, and built to last. Classic or contemporary, we help create the perfect piece for your home.

Pillar Structures

We design and craft custom cast stone pillars that bring elegance and structure to any space. Entryways, facades, or interior accents, our pillars are built to complement a wide range of architectural styles.

Bespoke Metal Fabrication

We create everything from elegant fireplace doors and stair railings to unique, client-driven metal features. No idea is too detailed or ambitious — if you can imagine it, we can build it.

Window Surrounds

Our surrounds add depth, character, and a refined finish to both traditional and modern exteriors. Each piece is tailored to your window dimensions and aesthetic preferences, with a variety of profiles and finishes available.
